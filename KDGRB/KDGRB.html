Big Book of R&B Songs

Tab City brings this collection of over 150 R&B, Funk, Blues, Disco and outrageous favorites from the early days thru the present with a focus on the classics. Nope not everything is in here and what is here has a bit of a New Orleans bent to it.  The book may be better suited for bringing these songs into bands rather than opening up and playing in front of a campfire, but see how it goes! The good thing is most of this is pretty playable given a few tries. Not too hard to pick up. Listen to the songs on YouTube or your favorite music streaming source to listen and follow along to the words and changes. The nice thing is each song fits on one page so you won’t be flipping back and forth. Usually riffs are notated with just the names of the notes, not real tablature except in a few places. So you don’t need to be able to read tabs. Just find the notes on your guitar. Hey, I’m a keyboard player, so I don’t do tabs. Anyways, enjoy this collection as I have and you’ll take your band to concert level jamming in no time. Music is from:
